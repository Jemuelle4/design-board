Problem Statement

How might we design an intuitive and efficient form section that assists University of Washington administrators in accurately inputting financial data to ensure seamless transaction processing across purchasing, payments, reimbursements, and HR/payroll actions?

Challenges

  1. Limited knowledge of FDM

    The FDM is a new concept at the University of Washington. Our team needed to learn how it works, and how we can teach and guide administrators of this new concept.

  2. No access to backend system code that connects to FDM database

  3. The FDM module must be implemented in new and existing forms
